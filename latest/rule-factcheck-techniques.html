Factcheck (Techniques)¶

This compares the object’s production dates with the object’ techniques, and identifies any cases where a technique is stated that did not exist at the time of the object’s known production. This could indicate:

  • A typo in dates

  • A production date range given that could be more tightly specified (e.g. if a technique was only available from a certain date, this sets the earliest bound of the date range, rather than a century or decade range being used)
